Getting Started

Set Up Your Society In Four Simple Steps

01

Create Your Society

Enter your society details, upload the logo, configure the address, registration information and basic settings to create your workspace.

02

Add Buildings, Wings & Flats

Organize your property structure by creating buildings, assigning wings, adding floors and registering every flat within the society.

03

Import Residents & Owners

Add owners, tenants and family members individually or import them in bulk. Assign each resident to their respective flat and manage user access.

04

Configure Maintenance & Go Live

Create maintenance charges, billing cycles, payment methods, notices and complaint categories. Once configured, invite residents to start using the platform.
